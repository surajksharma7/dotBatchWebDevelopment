biography

linus Torvalds is a Finnish-American software engineer who is the creator and principle developer of te linux kernal, which is the kernal for a number of operating system. he also created the version control system Git. Torvalds was born on december 28, 1969 in Helsinki, Finland. He studied computer science at the university of helsinki, and he developed the Linux kernal as a student project in 1991. Since then, the linus kernal has become one of the most widely used kernal in the world, powerinf everything from smartphones to supercomputer.
